Tequila Sunrise

Description

This pretty drink with layers of orange juice and grenadine is delicious on a hot day. The fact that the ratio of juice to tequila is high makes it a good choice for a brunch cocktail.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 fluid ounces resposado tequila
  • 3/4 cup freshly squeezed orange juice
  • ice cubes
  • 1.5 fluid ounces Grenadine Syrup
  • 1 slice of orange, for garnish
  • 1 maraschino cherry, for garnish

Steps

  1. Stir or shake together tequila and orange juice.
  2. Fill a chilled 12 ounce glass with ice cubes; pour in orange juice mixture.
  3. Slowly pour in the grenadine, and allow it to settle to the bottom of the glass (be patient).
  4. Garnish with a slice of orange, and a maraschino cherry.
